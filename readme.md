## Who We Are
**Orchid Hospitality Consulting Limited** is a talent management and job creation firm dedicated to supporting human capital development in the hospitality industry.  
We assist our clients to seek, nurture, develop, and retain top talent.

---

## How We Do It
We provide end-to-end services to ensure excellence in hospitality operations:

- **Information Gathering:** Collect and analyze all relevant information for vacant positions; prepare and modify job descriptions.  
- **CV Searching & Job Matching:** Identify and refer suitable candidates.  
- **Operational Diagnosis:** Detect problems or symptoms of failed operations.  
- **A–Z Operations & Event Management:** Oversee comprehensive operations and events.  
- **Standards Assessment:** Diagnose practices and conduct proper assessments to identify areas needing improvement.
